# ftp.elf.stuba.sk (now Slovak Antivirus Center)

ftp.elf.stuba.sk shut down and Slovak Antivirus Center took over, basically deleting way too many old and useful versions of compression utilities.
This is their selection of compression utilities from May 2024, the final known copy before SAC took over.
Under normal circumstances i would have dat'ed the inside of the zip files, but in the case of archivers, one might run into chicken-and-egg situations on really old platforms.
The .dat is in DOScenter format, so use RV 3.7.4 or later (unless you are self flagellant enough to use DOScenter itself).
Index files in the formats of 0index, files.bbs and descript.ion are also provided, so you can in fact plug and play them into that backup of your old BBS that you keep a backup of, just out of nostalgia
