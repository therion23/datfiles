## Fighting Fantasy
Origin: Various
* The legendary books originally by Puffin Games. Complete at the time of dat'ing.
## Paranoia (folder)
Origin: Various
* West End Games, later Mongoose. Separated into editions. The entire Perfect Edition, a few other books and some bonus material missing.
## Shadowrun (folder)
Origin: Various
* FASA, then FunPro, now CGL. 6th edition and Novels are incomplete due to lack of a proper catalogue, the rest are as good as complete (only two titles listed on Wikipedia missing).
